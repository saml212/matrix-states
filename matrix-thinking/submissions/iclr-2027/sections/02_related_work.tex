\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Descriptive rank measurement (the primary foil).}
    \citet{nazari2026rank}; \citet{sun2026staterank}.
    Same effective-rank instrument, uncontrolled $K$, no necessity
    theorem, no causal intervention. We are the mirror image: controlled
    $K$, provable LOWER bound, causal (train-time + eval-time)
    intervention, on the exact architecture family they measure.
  \item \textbf{\citet{nichani2025factual} (ICLR 2025
    Spotlight).} Closest prior theoretical construction --- rank-$m$
    existence proof under DISCRETE ARGMAX decoding (rank-1 recovers
    $\approx d$ associations under argmax). Motivates why our readout is
    pinned to exact-continuous cosine recovery, never argmax.
  \item \textbf{\citet{schlag2021linear} (ICML 2021).}
    Origin of ``linear-attention state as associative memory'' ---
    fast-weight-programmer lineage.
  \item \textbf{DeltaNet / Gated DeltaNet production lineage
    (\texttt{fla-org} kernel used directly) and DeltaProduct
    \citep{siems2025deltaproduct}.} DeltaProduct's ``rank'' is the PER-STEP
    TRANSITION matrix $A_t = I - \beta_t k_t k_t^\top$ --- a DIFFERENT
    object from the STATE's own rank $\mathrm{rank}(S_t)$ measured here.
    State this distinction in one explicit sentence (flagged in the source
    design doc as the single most likely reviewer confusion). Also:
    \citet{grazzi2025negative} (negative-eigenvalue state tracking)
    for the eigenvalue sign/phase framing relevant to the $h{=}21$
    depth-amplification discussion.
  \item \textbf{\citet{barnfield2026sharp}.} Capacity thresholds
    for STATIC rank-constrained associative memories (no recurrence, no
    training dynamics). Our delta: the training-time force-rank ablation
    on a time-varying, streamed state, plus held-out-hop composition.
  \item \textbf{Based \citep{arora2024simple} (Thm F.1) and
    Zoology \citep{arora2023zoology}.} Based bounds state SIZE
    (decoding-agnostic); justify explicitly why RANK, not size, is the
    binding resource under a pinned linear-unbind readout. Zoology: the
    standard synthetic associative-recall (MQAR) benchmark family our
    probe grammar resembles.
  \item \textbf{M\textsuperscript{2}RNN \citep{mishra2026m2rnn}.} Closest
    prior-art paper --- matrix-valued recurrent state, permutation-group
    (S$_3$) composition trained at seq-len 128, evaluated near-perfectly
    (${\geq}99.5\%$ accuracy) at 512. Their own comparison table has a
    plain vector-state GRU matching the matrix model on this exact task
    (also ${\geq}99.5\%$ at 512; rank not shown load-bearing --- GRU only
    loses on general LM perplexity, a different axis); their Theorem 1
    (``the recurrence can represent all tasks representable by
    non-linear vector-valued RNNs'') is representational, not necessity ---
    no lower bound on vector-valued models and no causal rank
    intervention. Lead with what their own results concede, then state the
    three-way mirror. \textbf{Citation specifics independently re-verified
    against the fetched paper text for this submission} (both sub-claims
    confirmed verbatim; see \texttt{refs.bib} note).
  \item \textbf{Superposition / distributed representation literature.}
    \citet{elhage2022superposition} for the general framing;
    contrast with our EXACT-recovery requirement (superposition results
    typically assume favorable/sparse readout, which our provable bound
    closes off). \citet{plate1995holographic} (Holographic Reduced
    Representations), \citet{frady2020resonator} (Resonator Networks) ---
    VSA/HRR capacity tradition, scope explicitly as a DIFFERENT notion of
    capacity (SNR/bundling) from this paper's exact-rank framing.
  \item \textbf{\citet{okpekpe2025revisiting} --- the credible
    rival causal account, engaged head-on.} Their claim: Transformers and
    SSMs differ in optimization dynamics, not expressive power ---
    MQAR-style failures in Mamba/Hyena are gated by a narrow
    learning-rate window, not a hard capacity ceiling, and disappear under
    a fine LR grid. A hostile reading of this paper's own capacity cliff
    (\S\ref{sec:phenomenon}, $x_0{=}0.5455$) is that it too is an
    optimization artifact. \textbf{Rebuttal:} this paper's causal chain
    runs through forced-rank construction and eval-time SVD truncation on
    an already-trained matrix, not through any learning-rate axis ---
    the recovery ceiling lands exactly at $k{=}K$ regardless of how the
    checkpoint was optimized, a structural claim their account does not
    make or predict.
  \item \textbf{Frozen-QK \citep{dong2025frozenqk} --- nearest neighbor for the
    frozen-anchor-table mechanism (\S\ref{sec:mechanism},
    \S\ref{sec:the-fix}), distinguished on three axes.} That work fully
    freezes an entire softmax transformer's Q/K projections at init;
    induction heads still form, but at a real loss cost ($\approx$10\%
    WikiText PPL regression). This paper's frozen anchor table differs:
    (a) additive, not full-freeze --- a tuned-weight blend into an
    otherwise fully-trained key path, not a projection replacement;
    (b) the blend's own gain is loss-neutral by construction (the same
    architecture with the table zeroed is the baseline); (c) it targets a
    bounded recurrent fast-weight state, not unbounded-context softmax
    attention --- the question (does a fixed-size state retain more) has
    no analog in Frozen-QK's setting.
  \item \textbf{VLA \citep{pandey2026vla} --- the geometric rival account for
    the capacity cliff, engaged against the dose-response exoneration
    (\S\ref{sec:dose-response}).} VLA's stabilized architecture
    (Kalman/variational update, spectral-norm-1 write direction) pushes a
    sharp MQAR-recall cliff outward, framing capacity as tied to key
    linear independence / state-matrix rank --- a geometric account, in
    tension with this paper's own direct coherence-injection result
    ($h4{=}1.0$ at every dose up to and beyond $d{=}64$'s realized
    coherence band, 10/10 cells). \textbf{The tension is stated, not
    buried:} VLA studies single-hop recall under an architectural
    stabilization; this paper studies relational binding plus multi-hop
    composition via a direct causal manipulation of coherence at matched
    load. It remains possible both accounts describe the same underlying
    fact from different angles (a rank-deficient key population is also,
    definitionally, capacity-constrained at fixed $d_{\mathrm{state}}$)
    --- this paper does not claim to have resolved which framing is more
    fundamental, only that its own causal dose-response manipulation did
    not find coherence-as-such to be the lever.
  \item \textbf{Our own prior, unpublished internal work (anonymized for
    review).} Not external work --- immediate lineage, released as part of
    this submission's own anonymized supplementary artifact rather than
    cited as a public reference. A from-scratch, bespoke-attention-encoder
    version of the identical rank-recruitment result, including the
    depth-amplification law imported directly (internal notes \S3) and an
    earlier negative result on a discrete-decoding readout that motivated
    the exact-continuous readout choice used throughout this paper. State
    explicitly: this paper TRANSFERS that result to a production
    architecture and, new here, discovers and fixes the exactness gap the
    synthetic construction never exposed.
\end{itemize}
