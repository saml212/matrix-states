\section{Discussion and Limitations}
\label{sec:discussion}

% Full content: NARRATIVE.md \S6. State every item undiluted --- this is
% the section a reviewer checks first for overclaiming.

\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{Small scale.} $d_{\mathrm{model}}$ in the low hundreds,
    $d_{\mathrm{state}}{=}64$, sub-1M--low-single-digit-M parameters. No
    claim tested at a scale where the write-time attractor might behave
    differently.
  \item \textbf{Single architecture family.} Vanilla, single-head,
    single-layer DeltaNet only. Gated DeltaNet (the actual Qwen3-Next
    production config), DeltaProduct's multi-Householder generalization,
    untested. $H{=}1$/single-layer was a deliberate gate-simplicity
    decision, not evidence of $H{=}1$-specificity (ReserveMH's
    generality result was run on the SYNTHETIC task only).
  \item \textbf{$h{=}21$ literal-depth decay remains unexplained and
    unfixed.} F-geo-3 demonstrably does not touch it --- a named,
    scoped-out mechanism (iteration-compounding), not folded into ``the
    fix works.''
  \item \textbf{The $K{=}32$ headline bar is not met.} The fix is fully
    admissible at BOTH $K$ ($K{=}16$ 3/3; $K{=}32$ 3/3 on the
    \texttt{n\_iter=20} escalation, zero fallback steps), but the
    pre-registered $\geq 0.5$ headline bar at $K{=}32$ is narrowly
    missed: $h{=}4 = 0.504/0.416/0.390$, mean $0.44$, 1/3 seeds at the
    line. State the miss exactly; state the attribution (outcome F,
    trained drift $0.9037$ in the pre-registered HIGH band ---
    cross-episode key STABILITY, not within-episode orthogonality, is
    the residual bottleneck); and state the follow-on (cross-episode key
    anchoring/stabilization) as FUTURE WORK with a measured target --- a
    named residual, not an open mystery, but still a limitation of what
    this paper demonstrates.
  \item \textbf{The $K\approx d/2$ structural boundary is observed, not
    explained.} The K=48 rider shows further breakdown and i-strong's own
    dimensional guard correctly refusing to run there; no mechanism
    account beyond dimension-counting is offered.
  \item \textbf{Scale-transfer to a full pretrained LLM is unknown.}
    Wave 2 establishes the truncation-damage phenomenon generalizes to
    naturalistic LM pretraining at small scale (descriptive+
    interventional tier only) --- does NOT establish whether the
    write-geometry attractor persists, weakens, or strengthens at
    production LLM scale. Explicit open question, not implied by
    extrapolation.
  \item \textbf{The admission-stack asymmetry, now footnote-level.}
    Because F-geo-3 makes the standard instruments tautological, a
    substitute pre-registered criterion applies to its seeds. With every
    substitute criterion passed at both $K$ (including zero fallback
    steps on the escalation cells), this drops from headline asterisk to
    footnote --- but still name both stacks and both realized pass rates
    wherever geo3 and non-geo3 seed counts appear side by side, because
    the two stacks filter different failure modes and their relative
    selectivity was never empirically equated.
\end{enumerate}
