\begin{enumerate}

\item \textbf{Small scale, partially addressed by a now-complete
scaling ladder.} The core causal chapters
(Sections~\ref{sec:phenomenon}--\ref{sec:results}) run at
$d_{\mathrm{model}}$ in the low hundreds, $d_{\mathrm{state}}{=}64$, and
sub-1M-to-low-single-digit-million parameters. A controlled
$14\mathrm{M}\to98\mathrm{M}\to392\mathrm{M}\to1.31\mathrm{B}$ scaling
ladder, held to a single fixed corpus mix and measuring the write-geometry
attractor only (not compositional recovery), is now complete at all four
points (Figure~\ref{fig:trackc-trackd}, Panel~A) and finds the attractor
does \emph{not} dissolve with scale --- it worsens monotonically,
span-fraction $0.248\to0.344\to0.389\to0.455$. Disclosure: the
1.31B-parameter rung self-terminated at its own internal timeout at step
$155{,}081/155{,}028$ of a planned $183{,}105$ ($\approx$84.7\% of the
token-matched budget) --- a clean, designed shutdown, not a crash, and not
resumable (no optimizer-state checkpointing existed for this run). A
dedicated plateau check at the final four logged checkpoints
($130\mathrm{k}$--$155\mathrm{k}$ steps) shows the reading flat, mildly
\emph{declining} over that window ($0.4584\to0.4554$, $-0.003$),
extrapolating to $\approx0.452$ at the full budget --- two orders of
magnitude smaller than the $+0.066$ rung-2$\to$rung-3 increment it would
be correcting, and in the direction that would shrink, not grow, the
reading. Harvesting at 155k rather than a $\approx$144 GPU-h cold restart
(for which no resumable state exists) was the adjudicated choice on this
basis. This item's core claim --- untested at production scale for
\emph{compositional recovery}, not just geometry --- stands regardless.

\item \textbf{Single architecture family, partially discharged.}
Section~\ref{sec:soft-fixes}'s architectural-robustness $2\times2$ shows
the attractor persists under both qk-l2-norm ablation (exonerated at
$n{=}3$, a within-noise null) and Gated-DeltaNet-style decay (a
direction-consistent trend that does not meet its own $2\sigma$
confirmation bar). DeltaProduct's multi-Householder generalization,
gating at production scale, and the full Qwen3-Next configuration remain
untested. $H{=}1$/single-layer (Section~\ref{sec:setup}) was a
deliberate gate-simplicity decision; ReserveMH's generality result lifts
that qualifier only on the synthetic task, not yet on the real-data
exactness frontier.

\item \textbf{The $h{=}21$ literal-depth decay remains unexplained and
unfixed.} F-geo-3 demonstrably does not touch it
(Section~\ref{sec:results}) --- a named, scoped-out mechanism
(iteration-compounding), never folded into ``the fix works.''

\item \textbf{The $K{=}32$ headline bar is not met, and its named
follow-on is fully resolved.} Section~\ref{sec:results} reports this
without softening: $h{=}4{=}0.504/0.416/0.390$, mean $0.44$, 1/3 seeds at
the line, attributed to trained cross-episode key drift ($0.9037$, inside
the pre-registered high band). The anchoring follow-on this residual
motivated ran to completion across four waves
(Sections~\ref{sec:the-fix}--\ref{sec:results}): a real,
independently-replicated $K{=}32$ $h{=}4$ gain ($0.4105\to0.61$--$0.71$),
a rejected entity-alignment hypothesis (Outcome C, confirmed twice,
immune to a rejected rescue attempt), a confirmed-by-ablation alternate
mechanism (a frozen, never-trained table matches or exceeds the learned
table's own gain --- constancy, not learned content, is the active
ingredient), and a measured, bounded operating regime ($K/d\leq0.5$
holds, $K/d{=}0.75$ fails as a cliff, not a smooth decline). This item,
open at an earlier stage of this project, is now closed. One
footnote-tier bookkeeping note travels with every seed count in this
arc: because F-geo-3 makes the standard key-Gram/alignment premise
instruments tautological by construction, its seeds are admitted by a
different, pre-registered substitute stack than the rest of the paper;
both stacks and both realized pass rates are named wherever geo3 and
non-geo3 seed counts appear side by side (full stack in
Section~\ref{sec:the-fix}).

\item \textbf{The $K\approx d/2$ structural boundary: located, shown to
dissolve, and its leading confound exonerated.} Section~\ref{sec:k48-rider}
carries this analysis in full --- the transition midpoint at
$d_{\mathrm{state}}{=}64$ ($x_0{=}0.5455$, 95\% CI $[0.5385,0.5513]$),
its dissolution at $d_{\mathrm{state}}{=}128$ across the identical $K/d$
window (12/12 cells at $h4{=}1.0$), the direct exoneration of
anchor-table coherence via controlled dose-response injection at both a
concentrated and a diffuse structure, and the super-linear (not
ratio-invariant) growth of load tolerance with $d_{\mathrm{state}}$
($x_0(80){=}0.6779$, excluding the pre-registered ratio-invariant band).
We do not re-derive that material here. What remains genuinely open,
stated plainly: the exact functional form of the super-linear growth
(neither an absolute-slack band $[0.718,0.739]$ nor a power-law band
$[0.768,0.837]$ is confirmed or excluded) and the precise
$d_{\mathrm{state}}{=}96$ transition point, where a full unlocked grid
finds no cliff through $K/d{=}0.9375$ but a non-monotonic curve too noisy
at $n{=}3$ seeds/$K$ for the sigmoid fit to localize (100\% of bootstrap
resamples degenerate); a dedicated pool-margin follow-up returned
degenerate readings at fresh seeds rather than a clean adjudication,
disclosed as an instrument-sensitivity finding, not a result (full
per-cell trail: Appendix~\ref{sec:appendix-tables}). Value-crowding ---
more entities packed into the same fixed value dimensionality, reducing
per-entity value-recovery headroom independent of key-side conditioning
--- is named as the design's own candidate explanation for what
distinguishes a Welch-bound-forced non-orthogonal table from one that is
not; it is not yet tested, and we do not let it read as more established
than that. Seed escalation at the noisiest measured group ($K{=}72$,
per-seed range $0.8426$--$0.9904$) is the registered next step, not
launched by this paper's own evidence base.

\item \textbf{Scale-transfer: corpus generality, the ladder above, a
production-scale signature with attribution left open, an installation
attempt blocked, and a separate fix-transfer wave now resolved.} A
smaller-scale check comes first: the truncation-damage phenomenon of
Section~\ref{sec:phenomenon} is not an artifact of the synthetic probe
grammar, and generalizes to naturalistic LM pretraining already at small
scale (Figure~\ref{fig:wave2-corpus}; descriptive+interventional tier
only, not premise-conditional causal). Beyond item~1's pure-scale ladder,
three further pieces of evidence bound how far this paper's findings
travel. First (Figure~\ref{fig:trackc-trackd}, Panel~B), a much
larger version of the same non-orthonormal write-geometry signature is
directly measurable in production-scale pretrained fixed-state models ---
RWKV-7 1.5B ($43.5$--$44.0$) and Falcon-Mamba-7B ($49.9$--$50.2$) --- but
a matched no-fixed-state negative control, Qwen2.5-1.5B
($46.0$--$48.5$), shows the same magnitude, so this signature is not yet
attributable to the delta-rule write mechanism specifically without a
sharper instrument. Second, a companion attempt to install the F-geo-3
fix mechanism itself inside a free-running-text LM (Track~B) is blocked
before any interaction claim can be tested: a duplicate-key
numerical-stability smoke overshoots its bar by $63\times$ with a
probative positive control, compounding the original $\beta$-uniformity
gate refusal; the selectivity-only readout that did complete is
inconclusive (a split verdict across corpora). Third, and distinct from
both: the separate constancy-suffices fix from
Sections~\ref{sec:the-fix}--\ref{sec:results} (the frozen/learned
key-anchor blend) was independently tested for transfer into a real,
from-scratch small-LM pretraining setting at 14M
parameters, then re-tested directly at 98M and 392M (28/28 cells,
zero gaps). At 14M, the deployed per\_token arm ($\lambda{=}0.58$) moves
write-geometry span-fraction in the \emph{destabilizing} direction
($+0.1955$/$+0.2273$, both CIs excluding zero), while an exploratory,
never-scaled global-vector variant stabilizes it
($-0.3319$/$-0.2308$). At scale, the deployed arm's destabilizing sign
\emph{persists}, attenuated: $+0.1133$ (openr1) and $+0.1011$
(wikitext) at 98M, $+0.0189$ (wikitext) at 392M, with 392M-openr1 alone
landing null ($+0.0065$, CI includes zero); no cell reverses to the
mechanism-predicted direction. The global-vector arm's 14M stabilization
does not survive transfer: it attenuates to near-zero at 98M
($-0.0580$/$-0.0338$) and sign-flips on WikiText at 392M
($-0.0124$/$+0.0190$). Val-loss neutrality passes in every tested cell at
every scale --- the free-of-cost half of the 14M result transfers; the
geometry half does not. (The 98M/392M magnitude comparison is descriptive
only: 392M ran a reduced 20{,}000-step budget against 98M's
Track-C-matched 67{,}547, confounding cross-scale magnitude with token
count by design; within-scale readings are the registered claims.) This
fix-transfer wave is a separate program from item~1's pure-scale
attractor ladder --- it trains its own 98M/392M checkpoints under a
frozen-bias intervention rather than measuring the untouched baseline ---
and its own compute is accounted separately in
Appendix~\ref{sec:appendix-tables}.

\item \textbf{Whether SGD reaches the $2\times2$'s jointly-sufficient
corner: a closed, three-part answer, not an open dependency.} The
``both together'' cell of Figure~\ref{fig:existence-2x2} rests on the
hand-built i-strong pin, a causal existence proof, not one SGD is shown
to reach on its own. The anchoring program (Sections~\ref{sec:the-fix}--\ref{sec:results})
was built specifically to test whether a learned mechanism can reach that
corner, and the answer is now closed rather than open: SGD reaches a real
behavioral gain, but not via the hypothesized entity-alignment channel,
and the actual channel (constancy in the blend arithmetic) is confirmed
by ablation with its own working regime now bounded. The practical route
and the causal existence proof are both true and neither substitutes for
the other; Section~\ref{sec:mechanism}'s closing subsection states this
precisely.

\item \textbf{An attempt to connect this paper's causal findings to
natural in-context multi-hop reasoning is bounded, not resolved, by five
harvests --- caught by the instrument's own pre-registered gates, not
silently reported as a finding.} A held-out-hop probe applied directly to
this paper's own checkpoints (the frozen-bias arms as the geometry leg,
the 14M--392M ladder as the scale leg) returned
$\mathrm{recovered\_frac@0.9}{=}0.0$ at every one of 78 cells and every
tested hop depth: the probe's own Stage-0 sanity floor fails
categorically, routing the result to PROBE-INVALID rather than a
licensed REFUTE, per this project's own rule that an instrument failing
its own sanity floor does not get to report a negative result. Two
structurally different natural-language reformulations of the query ---
one dropping the reserved marker, one substituting a far more
corpus-common relation-verb family --- failed identically, exonerating
surface form and promoting a task-familiarization instrument: continued
training on the probe's own bind/query structure (5{,}000 steps, 6
cells) followed by re-evaluation at 5 checkpoints returned the same
$0.0000$ floor at all 30/30 readings. A genuine dissociation makes this
more than a dead probe, not less: an independent vocab-space
cross-entropy readout, never trained on the geometric quantity the gate
measures, falls $21.8$--$46.4\%$ (mean $35.9\%$) over the same
5{,}000 steps the geometric readout stays flat at exactly zero --- real
task engagement co-occurring with a categorically invalid geometric
readout (the pre-registered $<$50\% pin for the strong ``invalid despite
learning'' claim is not met, closest miss $46.4\%$, reported as
PARTIAL-TASK-LEARNING-BELOW-PIN). This motivated promoting the
vocab-space readout to a primary, independent instrument in its own
right (12 new cells, 4 (corpus, arm) contrasts $\times$ 5 checkpoints).
Applying the pre-registered killer-prediction rule: 3 of 4 contrasts are
UNRESOLVED, an underpowered null at $n{=}3$ (the detectable-effect floor,
$\approx$1.5--1.7 loss units, is the same order of magnitude as the OFF
arm's own entire familiarization effect, $\approx$1.69) --- exactly the
outcome a pre-registered power check flagged as most likely. The 4th
contrast (wikitext $\times$ per\_token) was determinate at $n{=}3$ and
TRANSIENT ($\Delta{=}{-}0.4999$, CI $[-0.6241,-0.3758]$ at $c{=}2500$,
resolving toward baseline by $c{=}5000$), but a dedicated $3\times$
seed-extension wave testing this specific reading directly did not
replicate it (9 new seeds, mean $\Delta{=}{-}0.0746$, CI spanning zero)
--- and its own pre-registered batch-effect gate correctly flagged the
two cohorts non-exchangeable (OFF-arm variance ratio $4.47{>}4.0$) and
routed the combined reading to BATCH-EFFECT-FLAGGED rather than silently
pooling it into a confirmed or refuted verdict. Every determinate reading
found across all 20 primary checkpoints in the original wave was
negative-direction; none was positive. Because the tested intervention
is the arm's entire causal package (pretraining-era weight divergence
plus the frozen-bias blend firing on every familiarization step,
inseparable in what these checkpoints are), this bounds rather than
resolves the keystone question: does the frozen-bias key-bias
intervention change in-context multi-hop acquisition? A framing caution
this paper owes the reader, resolved by item~6 above: the deployed
per\_token arm's own geometry evidence moves in the \emph{destabilizing}
direction at every scale tested, so this question is about the
intervention's whole causal package, never a presumed stabilization
mechanism. Net, honest statement: the $d_{\mathrm{state}}$-space
geometric-readout construct is dead across all three surface forms and
training regimes tested, still routed to PROBE-INVALID, never REFUTE;
the promoted vocab-space instrument bounds any causal effect of the
arm's whole causal package at under 1.5--1.7 loss units at this seed
budget; and the one originally-determinate signal did not survive a
$3\times$ seed extension, whose own safety gate correctly refused to
pool it into a decision-grade verdict. No claim about in-context
reasoning, positive or negative, is licensed by any of the five
harvests.

\end{enumerate}
