\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

Capacity and exactness are separable, measurable, and separately fixable
properties of a fast-weight architecture's learned state. This paper is
the first to show that the gap between them exists on real text
(Section~\ref{sec:phenomenon}), to name its cause --- a two-part
necessary-and-sufficient mechanism, within-episode orthogonality and
cross-episode key stability (Section~\ref{sec:mechanism}) --- to
demonstrate a mechanism-matched structural fix for the first part
(Sections~\ref{sec:the-fix}--\ref{sec:results}), and to fully
characterize a completed attempt at the second: a real fix whose actual
mechanism differs from its hypothesized one, confirmed by ablation, with
its working regime measured and bounded rather than left open-ended.

The causal chain closes without loose ends. The bar is hit at $K{=}16$
(3/3 seeds); at $K{=}32$ the fix improves recovery $\sim$45$\times$ at
full admissibility, narrowly missing its headline bar for a cause
identified in advance (outcome F, cross-episode key drift) and
independently corroborated by the $2\times2$ existence proof
(Figure~\ref{fig:existence-2x2}). The named follow-on this residual
motivated --- learned cross-episode key anchoring --- ran to completion
across four waves, not future work: a real, independently-replicated
$K{=}32$ $h{=}4$ gain ($0.41\to0.61$--$0.71$) whose hypothesized
entity-alignment mechanism is rejected, whose actual mechanism is
confirmed by ablation (a frozen random table matches or beats the
learned one --- constancy, not alignment, is the active ingredient), and
whose operating regime is now measured: it holds at $K/d\leq0.5$ and
fails at $K/d{=}0.75$, where both explanatory channels are active but
insufficient and the capacity curve is a cliff, not a smooth decline.
That cliff is itself a complete, located finding
(Section~\ref{sec:k48-rider}): pinned to $x_0{=}0.5455$ at
$d_{\mathrm{state}}{=}64$, shown to dissolve rather than merely shift at
$d_{\mathrm{state}}{=}128$, its leading single-variable confound
(anchor-table coherence) directly exonerated by dose-response injection,
and the resulting open question --- is load tolerance ratio-invariant or
does it grow super-linearly with $d_{\mathrm{state}}$? --- answered in
favor of super-linear growth ($x_0(80){=}0.6779$, excluding the
pre-registered ratio-invariant band), with the precise functional form
and the $d_{\mathrm{state}}{=}96$ transition point left as the paper's
own explicitly registered next step rather than assumed.

\textbf{Closing note on scale.} The phenomenon this paper studies is not
confined to the scale it is studied at: a controlled
$14\mathrm{M}\to98\mathrm{M}\to392\mathrm{M}\to1.31\mathrm{B}$ ladder, on
a single held-fixed data mix, shows the write-geometry deviation climbing
monotonically across all four points (span-fraction
$0.248\to0.344\to0.389\to0.455$) --- a positive result in its own right,
now precisely characterized through 1.31B parameters, with the one
disclosed shortfall (the fourth rung's designed early termination at
$\approx$84.7\% of budget) shown not to be load-bearing for the reading
(Section~\ref{sec:discussion}, item~1). A substantially larger version of
the same signature is present in deployed production fixed-state models,
not yet attributable to the same mechanism without a sharper instrument;
and a separate wave testing whether this paper's own fix transfers to a
real small-LM pretraining setting at 98M and 392M finds it does not ---
the destabilizing sign measured at 14M persists, attenuated, rather than
reversing (Section~\ref{sec:discussion}, item~6). A companion attempt to
install the fix mechanism itself inside a free-running-text setting is,
independently, blocked twice on numerical-stability grounds --- named
honestly as a current boundary, not smoothed over.

We close on the honest scope, not beyond it: one architecture family
(partially discharged by a robustness $2\times2$, Section~\ref{sec:soft-fixes});
small parameter scale for the core causal chapters (partially addressed
by the pure-scale ladder above, never for compositional recovery); the
$K{=}32$ headline bar honestly missed, narrowly, with a named and now
fully-resolved cause; one mechanism, $h{=}21$ depth-compounding, left
explicitly unfixed; the anchoring follow-on's own fix bounded to
$K/d\leq0.5$ rather than open-ended; and an attempt to connect this
paper's findings to natural in-context reasoning that five separate
harvests bound but do not resolve (Section~\ref{sec:discussion}, item~8).
The anchoring arc's resolution should not read as bigger than it is: it
closes one named residual, cross-episode key stability, with a
simpler-than-expected mechanism; it does not extend the fix past
$K/d{=}0.5$; and it does not touch $h{=}21$ or full-scale compositional
transfer. Rank is recruited reliably wherever we tested it. Exactness is
not, its causes are now named and measured rather than assumed, and one
of its two named causes now has a working fix with a precisely measured
edge.
