\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

\begin{itemize}
  \item Restate the thesis in the paper's own final voice: capacity and
    exactness are separable, measurable, and separately fixable
    properties of a fast-weight architecture's learned state.
  \item This paper is the first to show the gap exists on real text, name
    its cause (a robust, non-orthonormal write-time key attractor every
    input geometry converges to), and demonstrate --- not just diagnose
    --- a mechanism-matched, pre-registered fix.
  \item Close on the honest scope: one architecture family, small scale,
    one boundary case ($K{=}32$) whose admissibility is
    \texttt{[[RESOLVE ENDING A/B]]}, and one mechanism ($h{=}21$
    depth-compounding) left explicitly for future work.
  \item DO NOT end on a generalization claim beyond what \S\ref{sec:discussion}
    licenses --- the closing paragraph is the highest-risk overclaiming
    surface in the whole paper.
\end{itemize}
