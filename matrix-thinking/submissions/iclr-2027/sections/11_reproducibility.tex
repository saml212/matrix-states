\section{Reproducibility Statement}
\label{sec:reproducibility}

% Full plan: REPRODUCIBILITY.md (companion doc, this repo). Keep the
% in-paper section compact: pointer + the artifact's headline property.

\begin{itemize}
  \item Code, result JSONs, and the full pre-registration/attack/audit
    trail (including the dated revision history of every design decision
    and the negative results run to completion under the pre-registered
    anti-Goodhart cap) released as a single artifact --- see
    \texttt{REPRODUCIBILITY.md} for the full release plan.
  \item Anonymous link at submission: \texttt{anonymous.4open.science}
    mirror, de-identified per the artifact's \S1.3. De-anonymized link at
    camera-ready.
  \item Every numerical claim in this paper has a matching entry in the
    released \texttt{EXPERIMENT\_LOG.md} record and a traceable
    pre-registration date/criterion (claim-tier appendix,
    \S\ref{sec:appendix-tiers}).
  \item Known caveats carried into the artifact, not hidden: kernel-path/
    precision pinning, the F-geo-3 admission-stack comparability caveat,
    small/uneven sample sizes in Wave 2's Q1/Q4 analyses, and the
    seed-count asymmetry underlying the eval-truncation ceiling numbers
    ($K{=}16$ $n{=}10$, $K{=}32$ $n{=}7$, $K{=}8/24$ $n{=}2$ each).
\end{itemize}
