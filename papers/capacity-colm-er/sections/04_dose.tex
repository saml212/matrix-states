\section{The Coherence Confound, Exonerated Twice}
\label{sec:dose}

\begin{figure}[t]
  \centering

The two scales of Section~\ref{sec:cliff} differ in more than raw size.
With $n = 107$ entities, the $d_{\mathrm{state}}=64$ anchor table is
forced non-orthogonal by the Welch bound: it measures
$\max\lvert\cos\rvert = 0.2842$ at construction and drifts under ordinary
training to a final-checkpoint band of per-load means $[0.373, 0.385]$
(recomputed from the archived cliff cells). % evidence: C5
The $d_{\mathrm{state}}=128$ table is exactly orthogonal at construction.
% evidence: C5
Table coherence is therefore the single most natural remaining
single-variable account of why the transition vanished, and it is testable
directly rather than by inference across scales.

\paragraph{Design.} Holding $d_{\mathrm{state}}=128$, $n=107$, and
$K=68$ fixed at exactly the geometry measured flat in
Section~\ref{sec:act2}, we inject controlled coherence into the anchor
table, freeze the table, and sweep the dose: $0.130$, $0.284$ (matching
the $d=64$ construction value), and $0.40$, the last exceeding the
$d=64$ table's entire realized training band. % evidence: C6
The injection structure is a registered co-primary axis: a
\emph{concentrated} variant confines the injected overlap to a rank-4
subspace, and a \emph{diffuse} variant spreads the same scalar dose across
a rank-48 subspace. Freezing is verified, not assumed: the pre-launch gate
requires the table's gradient path to be severed, and the recorded
coherence is bit-identical across all 10 checkpoints of every dosed cell
(a single distinct value per cell across the full 20{,}000-step run).
% evidence: C8

\paragraph{Result.} Recall stays at exact ceiling everywhere: $h4 = 1.0$
in 10 of 10 concentrated cells % evidence: C6
and 9 of 9 diffuse cells, % evidence: C7
at every dose, with no exception (Figure~\ref{fig:dose}). Hop-21
recovery across the 19 dosed cells spans $0.9963$--$1.0$, not uniformly
$1.0$, so the flat $h4$ row is a genuine ceiling of the measured quantity
rather than a saturated instrument. % evidence: C9
Per the pre-registered outcome semantics this is the strongest
exoneration the design can produce: directly injected anchor-table
coherence, at matched $K/d_{\mathrm{state}}$ and matched entity count,
held frozen for the full run, does not reproduce the
$d_{\mathrm{state}}=64$ collapse under either injection structure. What
this does not rule out is stated in Section~\ref{sec:limitations}: a
coherence profile that co-evolves with training, rather than being frozen
at initialization, remains a disclosed open variant.
