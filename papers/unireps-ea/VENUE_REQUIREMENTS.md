# UniReps — what the workshop actually requires (acquired 2026-07-09)

Source: https://unireps-2025.netlify.app/2025/call-for-papers/ (the 2025
edition CFP; the 2026 site was not live at acquisition time — the naming
pattern is `unireps-<year>.netlify.app`; RE-VERIFY every item below
against the 2026 CFP after the accepted-workshop list drops 2026-07-11).

- **Tracks:** Full Paper (9pp main text, archival proceedings) and
  **Extended Abstract (4pp main text excluding references and appendix,
  NON-archival)** — this submission targets the EA track. Camera-ready
  EA limit: 5pp.
- **Template (REQUIRED):** "use the NeurIPS LaTeX template" — the
  official NeurIPS Styles zip (2025 CFP linked
  `media.neurips.cc/Conferences/NeurIPS2025/Styles.zip`). This tree uses
  the NeurIPS 2025 kit (`neurips.sty`, 2025/04/02) from the
  `ml-paper-writing` skill's template library as the sanctioned
  stand-in until the NeurIPS 2026 styles ship — swap in the 2026 style
  file when the 2026 CFP names it. The NeurIPS checklist is NOT
  required for workshop submissions.
- **Anonymization:** all submissions must be anonymized (the style
  file's default submission mode — no `final`/`preprint` option — is
  the review build: anonymous, line numbers on).
- **Review:** OpenReview; decisions ~3.5 weeks after the deadline
  (2025: submit Aug 29 AoE, decisions Sep 22, camera-ready Nov 13).
