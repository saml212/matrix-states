\section{Scope of the Bounds}
\label{sec:scope}

\textbf{Bounded.} The single-layer self-iteration readout carries no
signal distinguishable from a broken correspondence at every scale and
corpus tested against two correspondence nulls
(Section~\ref{sec:geometric}); % evidence: C12-nullvalidation
intervention effects beyond 1.5 to 1.7 loss units are excluded,
% evidence: C5-power
and the transient is bounded in duration, direction, and weight.
% evidence: C7-replication

\textbf{Not bounded.} The hypothesis survives below the power floor and
through cross-layer hand-off; an expressivity ceiling
\citep{grazzi2024unlocking} may also contribute, though wave 4 points
toward the observable. % evidence: C4-dissoc
Nothing transfers beyond the DeltaNet family or bind/query grammar. A gate
without an enforcing code path is only a sentence; unblinded routing kept
zeros and transients from becoming headlines. % evidence: C8-teeth
