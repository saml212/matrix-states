# 7. Scope of the Bounds

**What is bounded.** Three claims are closed at the stated strength. The
specific geometric readout construct, one layer's terminal state applied h
times to a query, scored by exact continuous recovery, carries no signal
in this checkpoint family: not at any scale from 14M to 1.31B, not under
either prompt surface form, and not after task familiarization
<!-- evidence: C4-dissoc -->. Any causal effect of the frozen-key-bias
intervention on in-context task acquisition larger than 1.5 to 1.7 loss
units in either direction is excluded at the registered confidence
<!-- evidence: C5-power -->. And the one determinate transient is bounded
in duration (gone by checkpoint 5,000), in direction (harm), and in
evidentiary weight (n=3, with a pre-registered pooling gate refusing the
extension) <!-- evidence: C7-replication -->.

**What is not bounded.** The keystone hypothesis, that write geometry
matters for in-context composition, survives at effect sizes below the
power floor and through mechanisms the readout cannot see. The likeliest
such mechanism is cross-layer hand-off, in which one layer resolves the
first hop and a later layer the next; the readout tests single-layer state
self-iteration, an implementation strategy a multi-layer model never
trained on the grammar has no particular reason to adopt. The
vocabulary/geometry dissociation of wave 4 points the same way: the
information is present somewhere (the model learns the task), but not in
the form this state-space readout assumes. The bounds also do not transfer
beyond the DeltaNet family or beyond the bind/query grammar.

**Lessons for small-scale practice.** Three transfer beyond this program.
A registered gate without an enforcing code path is a sentence, not a
safeguard; the one wave whose chain lacked the code path ran its full grid
past a failed gate, and the fix, refusal at the process boundary, worked
twice on real failures <!-- evidence: C8-teeth -->. Mechanical outcome
routing fixed before unblinding is what kept a wall of zeros from becoming
an overclaimed refutation, and a harm-direction transient from becoming an
intervention-helps headline. And a replication gate that can refuse turns
"more seeds" from a ritual into a measurement: at 9.5 GPU-hours for the
whole program <!-- evidence: C9-compute -->, the expensive commodity was
never compute, it was the validity of each inference, and the gates are
what purchased it.

A geometric observable earned a downstream-validity test before further
resources went to measuring it more precisely. The test returned a null
with its assumptions visible and its escape routes closed, which is the
form in which a null is worth publishing.
