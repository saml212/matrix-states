\section{Three More Lenses, Briefly}
\label{sec:threelenses}

\looseness=-1 Three further incidents (rows 4--6 of Table~\ref{tab:catalogue};
numbers in Appendix~\ref{app:threelenses}): an
\textbf{uncentered covariance}, degenerate on the task's own
near-orthogonal targets, that failed a synthetic \emph{perfect} model
(the rescue de-confounds into centering plus a full-intertwiner
degauge);
% <!-- evidence: B1 -->
a \textbf{target with an ambient identity block}, under which a
causal rank experiment returned near-chance
recovery at every rank (zero in four of five groups; $0.17$ and
$0.08$ in the fifth) while the capped arms sat at the exact rank-$k$
ceiling $\sqrt{k/\dstate}$: undelivered by the
instrument, not failed by the model;
% <!-- evidence: B2 -->
and a \textbf{reference kernel invoked on the transposed side} of a
state convention, ``contradicting'' a correct recurrence at the
$\sqrt{2}$ signature of a transposed outer product, exonerated by a
closed-form hand computation.
