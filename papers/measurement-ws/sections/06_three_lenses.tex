\section{Three More Lenses, Briefly}
\label{sec:threelenses}

Three further incidents, detailed in Appendix~\ref{app:threelenses},
round out the catalogue: an
\textbf{uncentered covariance}, degenerate on the task's own
near-orthogonal targets ($ZZ^{\top}\approx c^{2}I$), under which a
synthetic \emph{perfect} model failed the production acceptance bars
(mean cosine $0.084$ versus $0.9996$ centered);
% <!-- evidence: B1 -->
a \textbf{target with an ambient identity block} that taxed every
rank budget, so a causal rank experiment returned zeros at every rank
while the capped arms sat at their exact theoretical ceiling
$\sqrt{k/\dstate}$ (37 of 39 cells within $0.07$): undelivered by the
instrument, not failed by the model;
% <!-- evidence: B2 -->
and a \textbf{reference kernel invoked on the transposed side} of a
state convention, ``contradicting'' a correct recurrence at relative
residual $1.40\approx\sqrt{2}$, the exact signature of a transposed
rank-1 outer product, exonerated by a closed-form hand computation at
$4.5\times10^{-8}$.
