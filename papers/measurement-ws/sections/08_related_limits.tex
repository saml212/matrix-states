\section{Related Work and Limitations}
\label{sec:related}

\looseness=-1 \textbf{Related work.} \citet{silberzahn2018many} and
\citet{damour2020underspecification} document divergent conclusions
from underspecified analysis pipelines; here the models are fixed and
the \emph{measurement pipeline} is the component being adjudicated,
with a per-incident decision procedure rather than a variance
demonstration. \citet{henderson2018deep} and
\citet{bouthillier2021accounting} quantify seed and tuning variance;
none of the six defects here is sampling variance (a transposed
convention or a wrong-population tolerance is bit-stable across
seeds), so variance accounting would have caught none. REFORMS
\citep{kapoor2023reforms}, \citet{sculley2018winners}, and
\citet{lipton2018troubling} prescribe what a trustworthy paper
discloses; this paper supplies the in-flight machinery such checklists
presume exists. \citet{nichani2025factual} prove the
argmax/exact-recovery gap of Rule 5; we report it firing live, on both
sides (\S\ref{sec:wronglayer}); the fast-weight testbeds follow
\citet{schlag2021linear}.

\looseness=-1 \textbf{Limitations.} All six incidents come from one program, one
team, one architecture family; we cannot report the rate a lighter
process would miss, and six incidents support no prevalence claim.
The catalogue is survivor-biased in one stated direction: a defect
that both the crosscheck and the falsifier battery miss would appear
in no record we could write. The economics
still favor adoption: Case I's two decisive diagnostic instruments
together cost under 2 GPU-hours against the 6.33 GPU-hours of training
their uncorrected readings would have discredited; auditing the lens
is cheap relative to the compute, and the claims, it protects.
