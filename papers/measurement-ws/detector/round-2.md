DISCHARGE.** Both judges cleared $\geq$90%-human (97/97, well above the
floor and above round 1's 90/99). But the "zero mechanical tells"
half of the pass condition, read literally, is not met either: both
judges' own scores sit below 100% specifically because of the cited
residual, and each names a taxonomy category (over-balanced/tidy-triple;
summary-of-a-summary-adjacent) for it — this is not the same as the
round-1 Judge A pattern (99% human, tell considered and explicitly
zero-weighted) where the score itself was undented by the cleared
signal.

At the same time, this round's citations are qualitatively different
from round 1's Judge B: no concrete "Fix:" instructions, both judges'
prose verdicts are unqualified "human-written"/"this reads human," and
both explicitly frame the residual as non-disqualifying rather than as
an open defect. This is closer to the "bounded-terminal no-actionable-
defect disposition" pattern the rank-recruitment-ws precedent names
(`../../rank-recruitment-ws/detector/round-1.md`, referencing a sibling
program's 68/90 split) — but that pattern is explicitly recorded there
as requiring **a coordinator judgment call**, not a call for the change
